Story behind the game - You are a Virtual Reality Item Collector, you will do anything and everything to collect items. You get Bots, Hackers, Admins, even the Internet Officials to help you out. How many Items can you get?

I'm an 22 yr old college student so this was just for fun, but future updates are planned along with a completely new game.


Item Collector was created for the purpose to learn and experiment with Javascript, but I want to thank the creator of Goomy Clicker for releasing the coding behind his game which has allowed me to do this!
